#### Creating Groups
1. Click "Create New Group" from the dropdown
2. Enter group details:

3. Start adding expenses immediately

#### Switching Groups
1. Click the group selector button
2. Choose from your list of groups
3. All data switches instantly:
   - Members
   - Expenses
   - Cash locker
   - Dashboard stats
